GoldenZone

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

GoldenZone is a product of Zero To Build LLC ("we," "our," or "the company"). This policy explains what data GoldenZone collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Location Data. GoldenZone uses your device's location to determine your hardiness zone, frost dates, and solar position calculations. Your location is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not store or share your location.

Camera and AR Data. GoldenZone uses your device's camera and LiDAR sensor for augmented reality sun path scanning. All camera and depth data is processed locally on your device in real time. We do not capture, store, or transmit any images or AR data.

Weather Data. GoldenZone makes requests to the Open-Meteo API to retrieve temperature data for growing degree day calculations. These requests include your approximate latitude and longitude. Open-Meteo's privacy policy applies to this data. No personally identifiable information is sent.

Garden Data. Saved garden spots, plant selections, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Purchase Data. If you subscribe to GoldenZone Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt — no personal information. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not collect usage data or behavioral analytics

Data Storage

All app data (saved spots, preferences, weather cache) is stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed storage. Deleting the app removes all data.

Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo — weather data (approximate coordinates only, no PII). Terms
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (anonymous user ID only). Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases processed by Apple. Apple Privacy

Children's Privacy

GoldenZone does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@goldenzone.site.
